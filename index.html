Dr. Jacob Thomas

A distinguished Electrical Engineer, visionary entrepreneur, and prolific social leader with a legacy of excellence spanning three decades.

A Portrait of a Multifaceted Personality

Dr. Jacob Thomas represents a remarkable confluence of knowledge, experience, and humanity. For over 30 years, he has forged a path of distinction across engineering, academia, and socio-cultural activism. After a notable career with New York City Transit, his pursuit of knowledge led to a Doctorate in Applied Science, earning him international respect as a visiting professor. A dedicated professional committed to community upliftment and diaspora empowerment, his contributions to cultural unity and the protection of expatriate assets are a testament to his enduring legacy.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

Beyond his engineering and academic pursuits, Dr. Thomas has made a significant mark as an entrepreneur in the hospitality sector, contributing to local tourism and employment in his native Kerala.

Owner & Operator

Kollam Beach Retreat & Beer Parlor

Successfully manages a premier hospitality business located at Mudakkal Papanasam Beach in Kollam. The retreat caters to both local and international tourists, playing a key role in promoting the region's tourism industry.
